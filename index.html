Hobart Weekend Itinerary

Friday – Late Arrival & Nightcap Drinks

Evening: Land at 18:55, Uber/taxi to accommodation.

20:00: Jack Greene – Great burgers & craft beer.

21:00: Evolve Spirits Bar – Whisky & cocktails with fossils.

22:00: Preachers – Beer garden with a bus you can drink in.

Saturday – MONA & Drinking

08:00: Breakfast at Pigeon Hole Café.

09:30–10:30: Salamanca Market – Local food & gin tastings.

11:15: Ferry to MONA – Hipster ride (choose the sheep seats).

12:30–15:30: Explore MONA – Dark, weird, mind-blowing art.

16:30: Shambles Brewing – Top-notch craft beers.

18:00: Lark Distillery – Tasmania’s best-known whisky.

19:00: Tom McHugo’s – Classic pub, great beers.

20:00: Hope & Anchor – Australia's oldest pub.

21:00: IXL Long Bar – Sophisticated cocktails & jazz.

22:00: Republic Bar – Gritty live music pub.

23:00–Late: Rude Boy – Fun, rowdy rum bar.

Sunday – Recovery & Exploration

10:00: Brunch at Machine Laundry Café.

12:30: Cascade Brewery Tour – Australia’s oldest brewery.

14:30: Explore Battery Point – Walk off the beers.

18:00: Dinner at Templo – Intimate Italian eatery.

20:00: Sonny – Tiny, vinyl-playing wine bar.

21:00: The Den – Cozy cocktails & Tasmanian spirits.

Monday – Final Brunch & Airport Beers

10:00: Brunch at Born in Brunswick.

11:30: Stroll along Hobart Waterfront.

12:30: Hobart Brewing Co – Goodbye pints before the flight.

13:30: Head to airport for 15:00 flight.
